About Us

We are a Pentecostal church previous located in boxhill known as Melbourne Christian Assembly. We are currently located at Hall entrance, St Benedict's Catholic Primary School Burwood.

Melbourne Christian Assembly formally known as Melbourne Pentecostal Assembly is a biblical based Pentecostal church in Melbourne Australia, and is the founding member and the first Malayalee Pentecostal Church affiliated to Indian Pentecostal Church of God (IPC) of the Australia Region.

In 1984, the Lord brought Pastor Thomas George & family to Melbourne, Australia. During that time the Malayalee community in Melbourne was only a few in numbers and there were no Malayalam speaking prayer meetings or churches in Australia. With the guidance and inspiration of the Holy Spirit, Pastor Thomas George started the first Malayalee prayer meeting in 1988 with few people at his then residence 15 Calverton Road Kealba, Victoria .That was the first Malayalam speaking prayer fellowship in Australia. In 1989, Pastor Thomas George pioneered the first Malayalee Pentecostal Church and had the first Malayalee Pentecostal convention in 1990. The church has since grown many folds and caters to the spiritual needs of many believers of the Indian Pentecostal Christian Community in Melbourne and other states.

Melbourne Christian Assembly (MCA), herein referred to as “MCA Inc.” is a biblical based Pentecostal church. MCA is a church that is affiliated to Indian Pentecostal Church of God (IPC) Australia Region. With the Holy Spirit’s counsel and guidance, Pastor Thomas George (Founding Pastor) had a vision for the church and MCA had its humble beginning on1990 with a congregation of just few members. The church has since grown many folds and caters to the spiritual needs of many believers of Indian Pentecostal Christian Community in Melbourne and other states, The mission of the church is to disciple the congregants in being able to serve in God’s kingdom by being empowered in solid biblical teaching which is operated in the Holy Spirit’s counsel and guidance.

Our values and belives

    The Holy Scriptures as the revealed will of God, the all sufficient rule for faith and practice. The Holy Bible consisting of sixty-six books including the Old and New Testament, is the inspired Word of God, a revelation from God to Man, concerning the will of God in all things necessary to our faith, conduct and salvation, so that whatever is not contained therein is not to be enjoined as an article of faith (Hebrews 4:12; 2 Peter 1:20-21; 2 Timothy 3:15-17).

    In divine trinity: one God, eternally existing in three persons, namely the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit (Deuteronomy 6:4; Isaiah 43:10-11; Matthew 28:19; Mark 12:29).

    That Jesus Christ is the only Begotten Son of the Father, conceived of the Holy Spirit, and born of virgin Mary (Isaiah 7:14; Matthew 3:16-17), that Jesus Christ died on the cross, and was buried and raised from the dead; that He ascended to heaven and is today at the right hand of the Father as our intercessor.

    In the Holy Spirit, the Lord and giver of life, who proceed from the Father and the Son; who with the Father and Son together is worshipped and glorified (John 14:16-17).

    That man was created in the image and likeness of God, but fell by voluntary transgression and his only hope of redemption and salvation is in Jesus Christ; that all have sinned and come short of the glory of God, that repentance is commanded of God for all, and is necessary for forgiveness of sin; that salvation, new birth and justification are wrought by the grace of God, and faith in the Lord Jesus Christ and the renewing work of the Holy Spirit (Genesis 1:26-31; 3:1-7; Romans 5:12-21; 6:23; 10:13; Titus 2:11, 3:5-7). And the inward evidence of salvation, new birth and justification to the believer is the direct witness of the Spirit, and the outward evidence to all men is a life of righteousness and holiness (Romans 8:16; 12:1-2).

    The Church is the body of Christ composed of all believers having various functions; and in the priesthood of all believers. And in the need for the fulfilment of His great commission. (Romans 12:4-8; 1 Corinthians 12; Ephesians 1:22,23; 2:19-22; 4:1-16; Hebrews 12:23; Mark 16:15-20; 1 Peter 2:9).

    In the ordinance of water baptism by immersion, in the name of the Father, and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, for all who repent and believe Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord (Matthew 28:19; Acts 10:47; Romans 6:4).

    The Lord's Table is observed corporately as one body in memory of His life, death and resurrection and will be observed by all believers, "until He comes" (Luke 22:17-20; 1 Corinthians 10:16,17; 11:23-32).

    In the second coming of Jesus Christ, the resurrection of those who are asleep in Christ, and the rapture of those who are alive and remain in the coming of the Lord, is eminent and is the blessed hope of the Church (Romans 8:23; 1 Corinthians 15:51-52; 1 Thessalonians 4:16-18; Titus 2:13).

    In the appearance of the Lord Jesus Christ in the heaven, the salvation of Israel, and the millennial reign of Christ on this earth. (Romans 11:25-27; 2 Thessalonians 1:7; Revelation 19:11-16; 20:1-7).

    That the devil and his angel, the beast and the false prophet, and whosoever is not found written in the Book of Life will be cast into everlasting punishment into the lake that is burning with fire and brimstone (Revelation 19:20; 20:10-15).

    In His promise and look for new heavens and a new earth wherein the righteous will dwell (2 Peter 3:13; Revelation 21:1)..

Vision

    To see all people accept Jesus Christ as Lord & Saviour.
    To see all people healed, delivered & transformed in Jesus Christ Holy name.
    To see all believers spiritually equipped & work towards the expansion of God’s Kingdom.

Mission

    To share the gospel of Jesus Christ with "ALL" people.
    To provide an atmosphere of worship that ministers to the entire family.
    To be involved in our local community.
    To support global ministries that are in line with our vision and values.

Ministries

our Ministries

Kids & Youth & Ladies Ministries

Kids and Youth is the childrens ministry of MCA church, it serves to raise a generation for Christ by teaching them moral and spiritual principles based on the Word of God helping them GROW in their relationship with Christ, learning to SERVE Him and SHARE Christ with the world. We are passionate about seeing our generation encounter the radical love of Jesus and see them take their place as sons and daughters of God. Our heart’s desire is to see a generation live like Jesus and become World Changers.

Ladies community seeks to ignite the passion for the Great Commission in the heart of every woman through small groups, mentorship programs, and outreach opportunities. Lifespring women aims to welcome women of all ages, connect them to God, equipping them to live out God’s purpose for their lives and eventually send them out to share the love of Christ with their communities and people around them.

Outreach Ministry.

Jesus Christ says in Matthew 28:19-20 “Go, then, to all peoples everywhere and make them my disciples: baptise them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, and teach them to obey everything I have commanded you. And I will be with you always, to the end of the age.”

At Melbourne Christian Assembly, we take an active part in evangelizing both here in Australia and India. Currently many of our brothers and sisters are actively involved in various types of outreach programs based on their availability during the week. One such ministry is street evangelism where we go into the streets, meet the people where they are, spread the good news of Jesus love and offer the hope in Him. These are the lives who are hurt, seeking for answers, feeling lost, confused, lonely etc. As Colossians 4:5 says, “Be wise in the way you act toward outsiders; make the most of every opportunity”,
